Traces for iOS remember where you've been

Requires iOS 9.0+, adapted for both iPhone and iPad

Features

Apple Maps Enhanced for Ourdoor Activity

  • Support all four orientations.
  • Zoom with single finger - tap and drag.
  • Traces 2 introduces the Star, enabling you mark important places.

Record Your Tracks

  • Record walking, running, cycling tracks, or bus and train tracks. In Traces 2, added Flight and Waterway mode.
  • Files are saved to iCloud Drive, and automatically synchronized to all of your iOS devices and Mac with the same iCloud Drive account.
  • File format is standard GPX. You can download these files from iCloud Drive and use them for other purposes.
  • Reduced GPX file size without compromizing on accuracy.

View Your Saved Tracks

  • Draw all your GPX files on a single map, with different types of tracks in different colors.
  • You can put your existing GPX files to iCloud Drive and they will be on your map.
  • Partial offline, 3D buildings and flyover view for some cities thanks to Apple.
  • Adapted for Chinese coordination shift.

Quick Instructions

Gestures

  • Slide from left edge: show the sidebar.
  • Zoom in and out: tap on the map then drag up and down.

Recording and Planing

  • Press the record button: start recording the locations.
  • Long Press the record button: enter planning mode.

Translation
