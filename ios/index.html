Record Your Itineraries With Ease

Traces brings you the most intuitive path recording interface. It shows all the interesting data on the screen: current speed, average speed, distance, duration, altitude and GPS data accuracy. It uses a novel track recording technique to reduce the amount of points in the GPX file.

Demonstrate Your Past Routes at a Glance

Traces puts all your GPX tracks in a single map. Different track types are marked with different colors. Next time you run, try exploring the unvisited places with Traces. Even if you have thousands of GPX files, Traces can handle it efficiently.

Star The Places That Matter

Traces 2 introduces the Star, which lets you mark interesting places such as landmarks, restaurants, or somewhere personal. There are two colors: red and green, which could mean places you want to go and places you've already been.

Beloved Wikipedia

We all love Wikipedia. Traces supports discovering nearby Wikipedia entries and you can star them easily, thanks to geonames.org.

Safely Stored in iCloud Drive

All files are saved in iCloud Drive, thus are synchronized across all you devices. You can either manage iCloud Drive on icloud.com, or on Mac or Windows.
