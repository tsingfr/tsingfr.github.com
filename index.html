Track Your Traces

At Traces, we believe less is more. The tracking panel is one example. It supports a compact landscape mode, which comes especially handy when your iPhone is mounted on the bike.

Traces currently supports seven activity modes: Walking, Cycling, Running, Driving, Train, Boating and Flying.

Recall Travel Memories

As many other GPS tracking tools, you can view basic track stats such as time, distance and speed. In Traces, you can also write note with Markdown. This could be a good place to write your travel logs!

Mark Locations with Stars

Google Maps already provides a very useful marker feature. Traces's marker feature takes it a bit differently. When you are running, you unexpectedly encounter a magnificent unnamed garden. With Traces, you can easily mark that location without pausing.

Similiarly to traces, you can write note on stars! Thoughts, notes, comments are all good for this.

All on iCloud

Traces is serverless. We don't own any kind of database or server. All of your data are hosted on iCloud. If you have plenty of data, consider buy more iCloud storage.

If you want to migrate your previous GPX files to Traces, just copy them to the corresponding iCloud Drive folders!
